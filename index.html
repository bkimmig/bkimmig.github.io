About Me

I am from Salt Lake City, Utah and am currently living in Portland, Oregon. In my spare time I enjoy reading and doing just about anything outdoors - especially if I can bring my dog Charlie.

I obtained by bachelors degree in physics. After graduation, I spent a few years doing research in Astrophysics - mostly stuff in the local galaxy involving kinematics and chemical compositions of stars. It was during this time that I found an interest in computer science, stats, and machine learning; eventually landing in a master's program for computer science at the University of Utah.
